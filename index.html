Independent Owner's Representative


Protecting capital for absentee property owners and investors during construction and renovation projects.


What I Do


Remote Owner's Representative

Independent oversight when you cannot be on site. I review progress, budgets, and contractor reporting to ensure your interests are protected.

  • Weekly project review
  • Photo & video analysis
  • Budget and timeline checks
  • Change-order review

On-Site Owner's Representative

Physical presence for higher-risk or higher-value projects requiring direct verification and contractor engagement.

  • Site inspections
  • Contractor meetings
  • Progress verification
  • Direct reporting to owner

Construction Consulting

One-off or short-term advisory services providing clarity before mistakes become expensive.

  • Scope & feasibility reviews
  • Cost benchmarking
  • Rehab planning
  • Second-opinion assessments

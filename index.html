Important disclosure:
The Consultant operates as an independent Owner's Representative and may provide similar services to multiple clients concurrently. All engagements are managed to avoid conflicts of interest, and no confidential information is shared between clients.

Remote Owner's Representative

Who this is for

  • Absentee property owners
  • International investors
  • Owners unable to attend site regularly
  • Projects where independent oversight is required

What this service includes

  • Weekly project review
  • Photo and video analysis (owner or contractor supplied)
  • Budget and timeline sanity checks
  • Change order review
  • Written weekly report to the owner

Pricing

  • USD $300 - $500 per month negotiable
  • Minimum 3 - month engagement negotiable
  • No travel required or, only on request by the Owner!

This service provides continuous oversight without the cost of full-time site presence.

On-Site Owner's Representative

Who this is for

  • High-value residential or mixed-use projects
  • Remote or overseas ownership
  • Owners requiring physical site presence

What this service includes

  • On-site inspections
  • Meetings with contractors and consultants
  • Progress verification against scope and programme
  • Independent reporting to the owner
  • Early identification of risks and cost overruns

Pricing

  • USD $1'500 - $2'500 per month negotiable
  • or 3% - 5% of construction budget negotiable (project-based)
  • Travel and accommodation billed separately or agreed in advance

Project-based engagements are common for larger or remote builds.
